Introducing: Oyinda

Having dropped her debut track last month, the piano driven flutter of Rush of You, London-Nigerian singer/songwriter/producer Oyinda has unveiled her stunning second offering, What Still Remains, and it's a more than worthy follow-up. Smoother and more refined than her debut, it's emotionally fraught lyrics are backed up by some of the impressive production of the year. This is a seriously impressive song.
